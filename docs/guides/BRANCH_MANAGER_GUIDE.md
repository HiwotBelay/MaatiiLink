# MaatiiLink — Branch Manager Quick Guide

## Sign in

1. Open your MaatiiLink URL (staging or production).
2. Sign in with your bank email and password.
3. You land on the **Dashboard**.

## Daily EOD (required)

1. Go to **EOD**.
2. Fill cash bands, complaint count, and notes.
3. **Save draft** during the day if needed.
4. **Submit** before the cutoff (18:00 Addis Ababa time).

## Incidents

1. Go to **Incidents** → **Report incident**.
2. Choose category and severity; describe what happened.
3. HO/supervisors are notified for critical items.

## HO directives

1. Go to **Directives**.
2. Read each active circular.
3. **Acknowledge** on behalf of your branch before the deadline.

## Service desk

1. Go to **Service desk** → **New request**.
2. Choose IT, Facilities, or Cash logistics.
3. Track SLA hours shown on each ticket.

## Need help?

Contact your district supervisor or COOP DX Valley support channel defined for pilot.
