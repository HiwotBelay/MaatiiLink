# National Rollout Plan

After successful pilot (Gate G5), expand MaatiiLink beyond pilot branches.

## Waves (recommended)

| Wave | Scope | Duration |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Remaining Addis branches | Week 1 |
| 2 | Oromia regional hubs | Week 2 |
| 3 | SNNPR, Amhara, other regions | Weeks 3–4 |

## Per-branch checklist

- [ ] Branch record created in **Admin → Branches** (`branchCode` matches core banking code)
- [ ] Branch manager account provisioned (min 12-char password)
- [ ] Champion completes `docs/phase5/PILOT_TRAINING.md` (45 min)
- [ ] First EOD submitted same day
- [ ] Pilot flag set only during trial; **Graduate to national** when stable

## Communication template (email)

> Subject: MaatiiLink now live at [Branch Name]
>
> Your branch is activated on MaatiiLink for daily EOD, incidents, directives, and service desk.
>
> URL: [APP_URL]/login  
> Your username: [email]  
> Temporary password: [issued securely — not by email if bank policy forbids]
>
> Complete EOD before 6:00 PM Addis time. Contact [supervisor] for support.

## Success metrics

Same KPIs as pilot (`/pilot`):

- EOD on-time ≥ 90%
- Directive ack ≥ 95%
- Incident median response < 4h
- Zero Sev-1 open > 24h
