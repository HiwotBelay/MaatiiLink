# MaatiiLink — Problem Statement

**Team:** SABA CODERS  
**Client:** Cooperative Bank of Oromia — COOP DX Valley  
**Status:** Approved by team — May 2026

---

## Problem

Coopbank has digitized customer channels (Smart Branches, CoopApp, Michu, Merchant Nation for merchants), but **branch-to-Head Office operations** still rely on phone calls, WhatsApp, email, and paper. With **753 branches** and **~15,000 staff**, HO lacks same-day, structured visibility into daily branch operations, incidents, and policy acknowledgment.

## Proposed solution

**MaatiiLink** — secure internal web platform for:

- Digital end-of-day (EOD) reporting  
- Incident and exception logging  
- HO directives with proof of acknowledgment  
- Internal service desk (IT, facilities, cash logistics)  
- Supervisor compliance dashboard  
- Audit trail for compliance  

## Out of scope (v1)

- Core banking / ledger  
- Merchant Nation (merchant acquisition)  
- Customer mobile apps
