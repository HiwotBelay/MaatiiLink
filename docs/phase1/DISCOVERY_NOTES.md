## Questions — branch manager

1. How do you send **end-of-day** information to district/HO today? (paper, Excel, WhatsApp, system name?)
2. What fields do you report every day? (cash, complaints, anomalies, staff?)
3. What time is the **deadline** for EOD?
4. How do you report **incidents** (fraud, system down, cash shortage)?
5. How do you receive **HO circulars**? How do you prove the branch read them?
6. How do you request **IT or facilities** help today?
7. What would make you **use a new app daily**? What would make you ignore it?

## Questions — supervisor

1. How many branches do you oversee?
2. How do you know which branches **missed EOD**?
3. What do you do when a **critical incident** happens?
4. What dashboard numbers do you need every morning?

## Questions — HO operations

1. What reports do you **wait for** from branches?
2. What causes **audit or NBE** findings related to branch reporting?
3. Who should **publish directives** in the system?
